The Orc that started it all!

Torgarr

The visionary behind The Laughing Boar, a tavern that's more than just a dining place; it's the heart of camaraderie in the city. With a presence as commanding as his laughter, Torgarr, an orc of considerable stature, transformed his dream into the beloved hub of the community. Dressed in his work-stained tunic and an amulet symbolizing his journey, he welcomes everyone with open arms and a boisterous "Come in, come in! There's always room for one more at The Laughing Boar!" His establishment, known for hearty meals and warm atmospheres, is a testament to the power of hospitality and the joy of shared laughter, making every patron feel at home. Torgarr's story is a beacon of how passion and a welcoming spirit can create a place where everyone belongs, lighting up the city one smile at a time.
